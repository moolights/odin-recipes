Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Description

You should know what this is. Chicken that's breaded, fried, then baked with sauce and cheese. Slap it on top a plate of spaghetti and boom.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Bake chicken with sauce and cheese
  3. Plate spaghetti and top with chicken
